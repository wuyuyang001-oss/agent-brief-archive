English overview

ARGUS benchmarks post-hoc uncertainty quantification for computer-use agents that turn vision-language model predictions into executable GUI clicks. The paper evaluates a 27-method open-weight matrix over four VLM agents and four GUI grounding datasets, plus an eight-method closed-source-compatible matrix across three vendors where logits, hidden states, and attention may be unavailable.

The main result is practical rather than cosmetic: UQ rankings transfer reasonably well within a fixed model and dataset family, but they become fragile across model classes, interfaces, and observability regimes. Hidden-state and density estimators are among the most stable choices when internals are available; for black-box systems, sampling, Focus-style signals, P(True), and verbalized confidence are useful but should be re-ranked on the target model.

The paper also evaluates conformal click regions for executable actions. Locally weighted disks can substantially shrink click radii under matched calibration, but coverage degrades when the calibration and deployment distributions diverge. The key limitation is scope: the benchmark focuses on single-step GUI grounding and post-hoc UQ, so multi-step planning, tool recovery, and long-horizon agent failure modes remain open.

对应中文翻译

ARGUS 评测的是 computer-use agents 的后验不确定性量化：这些 agent 会把视觉语言模型的预测转成可执行的 GUI 点击。论文在 4 个开放权重 VLM agent 与 4 个 GUI grounding 数据集上比较 27 种 UQ 方法，并在 3 家闭源模型供应商上比较 8 种兼容黑盒接口的方法，因为这些接口通常拿不到 logits、hidden states 或 attention。

核心结论非常工程化：在固定模型和相近数据集内，UQ 方法排序有一定可迁移性；但一旦跨模型类别、接口形态或可观测性 regime，排序就会变脆弱。当能访问内部状态时，hidden-state 与 density estimator 通常更稳定；黑盒系统中，sampling、Focus 类信号、P(True) 和 verbalized confidence 有用，但最好在目标模型上重新排序。

论文还评估了可执行动作的 conformal click regions。校准与部署分布匹配时，局部加权 disk 可以显著缩小点击半径；但遇到分布错配时覆盖率会下降。主要局限也很清楚：它聚焦单步 GUI grounding 与 post-hoc UQ，还没有覆盖多步规划、工具恢复和长程 agent 失效模式。

方法、创新点、主要结论与局限

核心方法

把 GUI grounding 输出视为可执行坐标/动作，并比较 logit、sampling consistency、hidden-state/density、attention、P(True)、verbalized confidence 与 split conformal prediction 等 post-hoc UQ 方法。

创新点

同时覆盖开放权重和闭源接口，把“能否访问模型内部状态”纳入 benchmark 设计；并用 ranking transfer、cross-tier transfer 和 conformal click disk 评估 UQ 是否可迁移。

实验结论

同一模型内的 UQ 排序较稳定，跨模型/接口则明显下降；开放权重下 hidden-state/density family 表现更稳，闭源场景不能简单照搬开放模型排序，目标模型重排更可靠。

局限

任务主要是单步 GUI grounding；闭源模型只能使用黑盒信号；conformal 方法对校准/部署分布匹配敏感；长程任务、恢复策略和真实用户环境仍需后续 benchmark。
